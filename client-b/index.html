Outreach

How we approach outreach (why it works)

We open with neutral, professional, low-pressure messages—no hard asks and no disguised offers. Think of it like a first meeting: start a real conversation, not a proposal.

Once that initial trust is there (after the first couple touches or light engagement), we shift into a light nurture path with an appropriate next step. LinkedIn is a transactional platform, but building initial trust is the biggest step—this sequence keeps resistance low and lets qualified people opt in quickly without pressure.

Expectations

What to expect in your inbox

You’re going to be pitched. That’s a normal side effect of rising visibility—and it doesn’t automatically mean the person isn’t a potential lead. On LinkedIn, many people open with a pitch because it’s the only way they know to operate (which is exactly why we don’t). Some messages will be a fit, some won’t. What matters is the overall rise in relevant conversations and opportunities—the rest is noise.
